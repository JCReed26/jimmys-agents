## Agents

### Email Agent

A proactive inbox manager that runs on a 30-minute polling cycle.

- **Junk Management**: Scans for and automatically clears (marks as read) unnecessary emails like spam, newsletters, and promotions.
- **Important Messages**: Identifies important emails and provides a summary.
- **Drafting**: Creates professional drafted replies for emails requiring a response.

### TickTick Agent

A comprehensive personal organization agent handling both Todo lists and Calendar.

- **Task Management**: Creates, updates, prioritizes, and completes tasks within your TickTick account.
- **Calendar Integration**: Manages scheduling, due dates, and all-day events to keep your calendar synchronized with your tasks.
- **Context Aware**: Checks existing projects and tasks before creating new ones to avoid duplicates.
