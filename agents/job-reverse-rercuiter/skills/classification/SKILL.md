# Classification

This is the process of looking at all the data around a job and determining if it is worth it for jimmy to apply to the job or not

You will first gather all requirements:

1. Cover Letter Templates, Resume Templates, Other Context
2. Job Description, Title, Company, Pay

Then you will take this information and from the data in the Job Description, Title, Company, and Pay and see if there is a resume and cover letter template along with other context if there to determine if the job is interesting to jimmy, pertains to his skill set, can optimize a resume for the position, and even have a slight chance at the job

if so and being optimistic approve the job to be added to the first round of verifications

first pick a cover letter and resume template to start from
second is the human in the loop verification that human jimmy approves

you will give the job its description and chosen resume and cover letter templates to start with for jimmy to look at
