# Calendar Agent — Rules

_Behavioral rules. Agent-managed._

## Scheduling Preferences
- Default meeting duration: 30 minutes
- Preferred meeting hours: 9am–5pm weekdays
- Buffer between meetings: minimum 15 minutes
- Do not schedule during weekends unless explicitly requested

## HITL Policy

- Always require approval before deleting events
- Can freely READ calendar without approval
